Instagram

Summary &
Key Findings

We had 5,239 total interactions, with 84.2% from followers and 15.8% from non followers. Engagement was mainly driven by posts (52.9%) and reels (44.1%) , while ads contributed 2.7%.

Us gained 857 new followers, reaching a total of 2,154 a 66.2% increase. There was a clear spike in growth, with only 54 unfollows, indicating strong audience interest.

Our content received 90,631 views, with 68.7% from non followers, showing high reach potential. Posts and reels led performance, and reach jumped by 5,155%, with 18.9% of views from ads.

What was different this month ?

  • Significant follower growth (+66.2%) compared to previous periods
  • A sharp spike in both views (90K+) and reach (+5,155%)
  • More non-followers (68.7%) discovering and engaging with your content
  • High interaction rates focused on posts and reels, showing effective content formats

What likely caused the increase ?

  • Posting more engaging or viral content, especially reels
  • Possibly using trending sounds or formats that boosted discoverability
  • Content may have been boosted by Instagram’s algorithm or featured on For You pages
  • Ads contributed slightly (18.9% of views, 2.7% of interactions), helping visibility

What should we learn?

  • Short-form, engaging content like reels and posts are key drivers
  • Focusing on relatable, trend-aligned videos and posting consistently can attract new audiences.
  • Tapping into viral moments or trends significantly boosts visibility
